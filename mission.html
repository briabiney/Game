Our Mission

The purpose of this game is to spread awareness about dog pounds. Every day 100s of dogs are kept in cages and treated terribly we need to make a change and treat dogs better.

We made this game because of all the sad dog comercials about the pound. Usually those are the commercials that people cry about, but they never usually do anything about it. This game is to make them consider taking action
